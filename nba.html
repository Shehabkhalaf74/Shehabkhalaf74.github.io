GAME TIPS

NBA 2K22 offers realistic gameplay for anyone looking to pick up a controller and experience some friendly competition. However, if you’re interested in taking your skills to the next level, Up Your Game is the quintessential guide to becoming a better player. Through a series of videos and articles, you can learn crucial tools that will separate you from the rest of the competition. Below, you’ll find in-depth breakdowns simplifying the ins and outs of dribbling, shooting, defending, and more. Studying these topics will increase your gameplay knowledge and give you the upper hand overall. NBA 2K Gameplay Director Mike Wang and his team have designed myriad ways to be a potent threat on offense and defense, but it takes practice. While NBA 2K22 is an intuitive game, those who go the extra mile to master the minutiae will see their game soar to new heights.

DRIBBLE COMBOS WITH CHRIS BRICKLEY

Dribbling is a fundamental part of basketball, opening opportunities for you as both a scorer and as a passer. From between-the-legs to in-and-out crossovers, skills coach Chris Brickley wants you to know how you can use dribble combos to your advantage in NBA 2K. Brickley is no stranger to working with some of the biggest names in the NBA, helping offensive assassins refine their skills. Learn and understand the many ways you can up your game by incorporating these fundamental moves just like the pros.
